Welcome to Searching

Searching follows the story of two protagonists who embark on a magical journey to save the life of a loved one, and solve a mysterious disappearance, all-the-while having a blast at the same time through creating new friendships, rivalry and discoveries they hadn't image they would unfold

Join them on the adventure of a lifetime, defeating foes and finding friendship in unexpected places.

Kenny

Meet Kenny. Kenny, just 11, spends his days looking after his sick mother. Unfortunately, her health continues to deteriorate - it's up to Kenny to find a cure!

Hachiko

Hachiko is an Akita, and possibly the most remarkable loyal dog, whom after his owner's disappearance, continued to wait for over 5 years. Hachiko is fed up. He refuses to accept defeat. Somewhere out there, his owner required a his companion back again. Now he's on a mission, with his own companion to find his owner. Will his loyalty be rewarded with a reunion, or is there an ulterior motive behind this disappearance?
