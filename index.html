Welcome to Searching

Searching follows the story of two protagonists who embark on a magical journey to save the life of a loved one, and solve a mysterious disappearance, all-the-while having a blast at the same time through creating new friendships, rivalry and discoveries they hadn't image that would unfold. Join them on the adventure of a run-time, defeating foes and finding friends in unexpected places.

Kenny

His mother is dying. It is up to him to find a cure. Can he find it in time?

Hachiko

After his owner's disappearance, Hachiko waits for 5 years only for his return. He is fed up, now, determined to set out for his search.

Some Goblin

"Yeet yeet", "Ka-Caw Ka-Caw"

Will their hard struggles reward the pair with a reunion and a saved life, or is there an ulterior motive behind his disappearance?
