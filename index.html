Welcome to Searching

Searching is a tale of two friends embarking on a magical journey to save the life of a loved one, and solve a mysterious disappearance.

Join them for the adventure of a lifetime, defeating foes and finding friendship in unexpected places.

Kenny

Meet Kenny. Kenny is an 11 year old boy, who currently spends his days looking after his sick mother. Unfortunately, her health continues to deteriorate - it's up to Kenny to find a cure!

Hachiko

Hachiko is a Japanese Akita, and possibly the most loyal dog EVER. Hachiko's owner has been tragically missing for over a year, but Hachiko refuses to accept defeat - he has remained in the exact same spot he saw his owner. Will his loyalty be rewarded with a reunion?
